About Udant

Udant is a revolutionary digital platform transforming the agricultural supply chain by connecting farmers directly with buyers. Our goal is to eliminate middlemen, ensure fair pricing, and provide farmers with financial stability through pre-harvest contracts and real-time market insights.

By leveraging AI-driven analytics, transparent transactions, and a seamless contract system, Udant empowers farmers with the knowledge and tools to make informed decisions. Bulk buyers benefit from a reliable supply chain, stable pricing, and hassle-free procurement.

At Udant, we are building a farmer-first approach, ensuring trust, efficiency, and sustainability in every transaction.

How Udant Works

Udant simplifies the agricultural trade process by connecting farmers directly with buyers through a transparent, secure, and efficient platform.

1. Farmers Register

Farmers sign up, create their profile, and list their crops with details like expected yield, location, and availability.

2. Secure Contracts

Farmers receive offers from verified buyers and enter into pre-harvest digital contracts to lock in fair prices and ensure timely payments.

3. Hassle-Free Selling

Farmers harvest and deliver crops as per contract terms while Udant ensures smooth logistics and secure transactions.

4. Get Paid & Track Insights

Farmers receive guaranteed payments without delays, track earnings, and gain insights for better decision-making.
